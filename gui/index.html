About AEYE Health

Our eye-health application is designed to help you maintain optimal blinking frequency and reduce digital eye strain. Using advanced computer vision technology, we monitor your blinking patterns during computer use and provide intelligent notifications to remind you to take blink breaks when needed.

Digital eye strain affects millions of people worldwide, causing symptoms like dry eyes, fatigue, headaches, and blurred vision. By promoting healthy blinking habits, our app helps alleviate these symptoms and improve your overall eye health and productivity.
